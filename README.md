
📊 Hotel-Booking-Cancellation-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using Excel / Power BI)
🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze hotel booking data to understand the key factors influencing booking cancellations. This helps hotel management reduce cancellations, improve customer retention, and optimize revenue strategies for future bookings.

❓ Questions (KPIs)
What is the overall cancellation rate?
Which month has the highest number of cancellations?
Which type of hotel (City / Resort) has more cancellations?
Who cancels more bookings based on customer type?
What is the impact of lead time on cancellations?
Which distribution channel contributes most to cancellations?
Relationship between price (ADR) and cancellations
Cancellation rate based on room type and booking type
⚙️ Process
Verified dataset for missing values and handled inconsistencies
Performed data cleaning and transformation (data types, formats, null values)
Created calculated columns/measures (Cancellation Rate, ADR, etc.)
Built pivot tables / visuals based on KPIs
Designed an interactive dashboard using slicers/filters (Hotel Type, Month, Customer Type)
Combined all visuals into a single dynamic dashboard

📈 Project Insight
A significant portion of bookings get cancelled, impacting hotel revenue
City hotels tend to have higher cancellation rates compared to resort hotels
Customers with longer lead time are more likely to cancel bookings
Online travel agencies contribute the most to booking cancellations
Higher pricing (ADR) is often associated with increased cancellations
Certain months/seasons show higher cancellation trends
✅ Final Conclusion
To reduce hotel booking cancellations, management should focus on customers with high lead time bookings and optimize pricing strategies. Encouraging direct bookings through incentives and offering flexible yet controlled cancellation policies can help minimize revenue loss.
